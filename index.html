FAQ

Questions answered

How long does it take to build my site?
Normal lead time is 5 working days from the start of production (after your 50% downpayment and completed customization docs). Need it faster? A Rush Order adds PHP 700 and gets your site ready in 2 working days.
How many revisions are included?
Every package includes 5 free revision rounds — 2 design revisions (layout, colour, style) and 3 minor revisions (text, photo swaps, small tweaks). Major revisions requested beyond these rounds are charged at 30% of your package price.
What's the payment structure?
A 50% non-refundable downpayment is required before we begin production — this officially books your slot and starts your lead time. The remaining 50% is due upon your final approval before the site goes live.
Do you use templates?
Never. Every site is built from scratch, designed specifically for you. No two Kneel Studios sites look the same — your event deserves its own identity, not a recycled layout.
Can I add a password to keep my site private?
Yes! The Password-Protected Site add-on is PHP 199. Only guests who know the password can access your site — perfect if you want to keep things intimate or surprise your guests.
What is the Find My Invitation / Exclusive RSVP add-on?
For PHP 500, we build a search feature where each guest can look up their name and find their personalized invitation — or RSVP privately. It's an elegant, high-end touch your guests will remember.
How do I start an order?
Fill out our order form (linked below), then message us on our socials — Facebook or Instagram @kneelstudios — with "order form done". We'll take it from there!
