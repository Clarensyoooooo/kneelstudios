Our Story

A Love Built on Timing

Four years ago, at a crowded coffee shop in Katipunan, Jim asked to share Edra's table because there were no other seats available. That simple interaction ended with a polite goodbye and nothing more.

Two years later, fate decided it was time. They bumped into each other again at a mutual friend's gig. Recognizing the familiar face from the coffee shop, Jim finally introduced himself properly. This time, the conversation didn't end.

From late-night drives to quiet weekends at home, they realized they had found their missing piece. After years of shared dreams and building a life side by side, they're ready for forever.
