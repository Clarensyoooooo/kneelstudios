Our Story

Our Serendipitous
Love Story

Nikko and Karrell first crossed paths during their early college days. While studying for the same major, they often ended up in the same study groups. Back then, they were nothing more than acquaintances who shared class notes and the occasional joke about their demanding professors.

Fast forward a few years to 2021. A mutual friend's weekend beach trip to La Union brought them back together. Away from the pressure of exams and deadlines, they finally had the chance to really talk. Over late-night bonfires and surfing wipeouts, they discovered a shared love for spontaneous adventures and iced coffee.

What started as casual hangouts quickly turned into intentional dates. Nikko admired Karrell's unwavering optimism and drive, while Karrell found a steady, comforting presence in Nikko's quiet confidence. Through career changes, personal milestones, and countless road trips, they became each other's greatest supporters.

On a chilly morning hike up Mt. Pulag, just as the sun crested over the sea of clouds, Nikko asked the easiest question of Karrell's life. With zero hesitation, she said yes.

Five beautiful years later, they are ready to embark on their greatest adventure yet.
